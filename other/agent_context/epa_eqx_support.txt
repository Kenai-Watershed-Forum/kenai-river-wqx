I spoke the support person at wqx@epa.gov, Kevin Christian. I will write down here some of our conversation and have it be incorporated in to to-do's and documentation for this project.

- Our existing data in WQX appear to be organized at the "Activity" level. He recommends instead organizing it at the "Results" level. E.g., each sampling activity (unique time/place) is associated with a set of results (parameters).

- It is not necessarily "wrong" as is, but as the number of observations increase, performance decreases

- Addressing this involves using the "Review" function in WQX to access all existing results and re-defining them as "Results" rather than activities

- This task is likely best performed in the separate QA/QC quarto book previously referenced and linked in AGENTS.md
